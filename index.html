News

To start this adventure, the five teams of novice sailors will train together in the art of marine science and sailing and begin their search for vessels all while we begin filming. Sail training will take place at a facility in Florida and the teams will complete course work in Marine Sciences at our facilities in Eastern Carolina.

Upon receipt of sponsorship, the vessels will be purchased and the five teams will begin their efforts to set sail by purchasing, rehabbing and launching the vessels. The students will then set sail around the Great Loop, all the while campaigning their vessel and creating videos to promote their adventure to an audience who through social media, Patreon and YouTube will follow them. The crews will fund their voyage though their viewers support of their team of choice by contributing patronage and supplies as deemed necessary by the team and communicated through social media. The team who after a season garners the most patronage and logs the most sea miles wins a cash prize and stardom as The First Great Looper, but it is our hope that all teams will sail on with established YouTube followers and Patrons to sail anywhere they wish in the world and continue to live the Mobile Lifestyle.

We are so excited to begin this adventure with you...
